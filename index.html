Who's The Girl?

Hi, I'm Jane! I was in the Hospitality industry working in the Pastry Department for many years before diving into the world of code. I feel like both worlds are no different from one another, except having a sweet treat after your done baking that you can eat. You have a sweet functioning webpage or app to display all of your work to make your life convenient...well, hey, that's the end goal at least! I love creating things from the ground up. It's so exciting to see a product that you started from the beginning turn into something that go beyond your expectations! I created my first webpage when I was in high school by creating fan pages for my favorite bands. Recently, I graduated at New York Code and Design Academy's Web Development Intensive program. I have learned that creating webpages and apps can go beyond simple HTML (and hosting them on Angelfire).
