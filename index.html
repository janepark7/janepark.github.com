About Me

Hi, I'm Jane! I was Baker and Pastry Chef for many years before diving into the world of code. I feel like both worlds are no different from one another, except having the end result being a sweet treat, you have a sweet functioning webpage or app to display all of your work to make your life convenient...well, hey, that's the end goal at least!

I love creating things from the ground up. It's so exciting to see a product that you started from the beginning turn into something that go beyond your expectations! I created my first webpage when I was in high school by making fan pages for my favorite bands. Recently, I graduated from New York Code and Design Academy's Web Development Intensive program. I have learned that creating webpages and apps can go above and beyond simple HTML (and hosting them on Angelfire).
